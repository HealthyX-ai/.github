# Code of Conduct

> **HealthyX-ai Organization**
> Effective Date: 2026-03-01

---

## Our Commitment

HealthyX builds tools that affect real patients and real clinicians. The quality of what we build depends directly on the quality of how we work together. Every person contributing to this organization — employee, contractor, or partner — is expected to uphold the standards in this document.

This is not a formality. It is how we operate.

---

## Standards

### What we expect

- Communicate directly and respectfully — say what you mean, and mean what you say
- Give and receive feedback on the work, not the person
- Acknowledge when you are wrong and move on without drama
- Ask questions when you do not understand something — that is always the right call
- Protect the confidentiality of patient data, client information, and unreleased product details
- Raise concerns through the right channel rather than venting publicly

### What we do not tolerate

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks in any form
- Discrimination based on race, ethnicity, gender, religion, sexual orientation, disability, age, or national origin
- Sharing PHI, client data, or confidential company information outside authorized channels
- Committing secrets, credentials, or sensitive data to any repository
- Retaliating against anyone who raises a concern in good faith
- Using this organization's resources for work outside its authorized scope

---

## Healthcare-Specific Obligations

Because HealthyX processes Protected Health Information (PHI), contributors have additional obligations beyond standard conduct:

- Never include patient data, client data, or PHI in code, commits, issues, pull requests, or comments
- Never share repository access credentials or personal access tokens
- Report any suspected PHI exposure immediately — do not wait, do not self-investigate
- Follow all applicable HIPAA obligations as outlined in your agreement with HealthyX

A PHI incident is not treated as a conduct violation when reported in good faith. It is treated as a conduct violation when concealed.

---

## Reporting

If you observe a violation of this Code of Conduct — or experience one yourself — report it to the HealthyX organization administrators directly.

Reports are treated confidentially. Retaliation against anyone who reports in good faith is itself a violation of this Code and will be addressed accordingly.

---

## Enforcement

Violations are reviewed by HealthyX organization administrators. Depending on severity and context, responses may include a private correction, a formal warning, temporary suspension of access, or permanent removal from the organization.

For confirmed PHI or security incidents, HealthyX's incident response procedures apply in addition to any conduct response.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all activity within the HealthyX-ai GitHub organization — repositories, issues, pull requests, code reviews, discussions, and any other contribution or communication that occurs here.

It also applies to conduct outside this organization when that conduct directly affects the safety, trust, or integrity of HealthyX's work or its people.
